# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-17

`YouTube Auto Ad Skipper` is designed to work locally in your browser.

## Data collection

- The extension does not collect personal data.
- The extension does not transmit browsing data to external servers.
- The extension does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or remote logging.

## Data storage

- By default, the extension does not store user data remotely.
- Runtime behavior happens in memory in your local browser tab context.

## Permissions usage

- `tabs`: used to detect and ping YouTube tabs.
- `scripting`: used to execute page-context checks/click logic.
- `debugger`: optional fallback click dispatch when standard DOM events are ignored.
- `alarms`: used for periodic background heartbeat checks.

## Third-party services

- The extension does not directly call third-party APIs.
- Any network traffic seen in YouTube pages is generated by YouTube/Google scripts, not this extension.
